Eve Schultz Fine Arts

Eve grew up in the thumb of Michigan surrounded by the animals on her family's farm and the shores of Lake Huron. Looking up to artists such as Teal Blake, Mark Maggiori, and Will James, Eve continues to progress as an aspiring self-taught artist. Her biggest hope is that others may see the fine details of the ranching and farming way of life through her art. Eve's medium of choice is pen and ink.
